### 1. Ставим 3 ВМ и на них ставим postgres 15 версии.  
![image](https://github.com/13-rus/Otus/assets/120638894/9d165161-865c-47e8-83de-ff38eddbc923)

/etc/postgresql/15/main/pg_hba.conf  
# IPv4 local connections:
